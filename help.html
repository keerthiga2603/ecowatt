About Us

EcoWatt is an innovative app designed to monitor solar panel performance in real-time. It helps users track energy production, monitor carbon emissions, and analyze solar panel efficiency.

Features of the App

  • Real-time energy production tracking
  • Carbon emissions tracking
  • Peak energy production alerts
  • Historical data and reports generation
  • User-friendly interface for easy monitoring
